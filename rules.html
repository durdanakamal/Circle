Our rules and guidelines

For lenders

1. For registration, you must provide proof of your ID, address details, and contact info.

2. You must ensure that your products are well-maintained and do not have any damage.

3. Ensure that the pictures of the product(s) you upload are clear and accurate of the product to be rented. If not, your product(s) shall be removed from our listing.

4. Provide an accurate description of the product(s) to be listed along with the retail price.

5. Rent price should not be more than 40 – 50 % of the actual retail price.

6. You can confirm the order only after the borrower chooses their preferred payment method: Cash on Delivery / bkash / card.

7. If your product gets damaged by the borrower in any way, they are responsible for its repair, cleaning and / or replacement costs. Otherwise, seek fair monetary compensation from the borrower. If there is a conflict, seek our help for mediation. Rest assured; we shall ensure you’re compensated for justly.

8. If the borrower is late in returning your item and doesn’t pay for it either, we shall do everything in our power to ensure you are paid fairly.

9. If necessary, you can communicate with the borrower too by sending a private message.

10. No rude language, bullying, or sexual harassment will be tolerated under any circumstances. If found guilty, your account will be revoked, and you shall not be part of our community anymore.

For borrowers

1. For registration, you must provide your name, proof of your ID, address details, and contact info.

2. During the rental period, you are responsible for the rented products and must ensure that they are maintained well. If you damage it any way (we understand accidents happen), you must bear all the repair, cleaning and / or replacement costs. Otherwise, you must pay a fair monetary compensation to the lender. If a conflict comes up, seek our help as a mediator.

3. Your order is confirmed only after you choose your preferred payment method of cash on delivery / bkash/ card and are approved by the lender.

4. If necessary, you can communicate with the lender too by sending a private message.

5. If your product gets damaged by the borrower in any way, seek fair monetary compensation from the borrower. If there is a conflict, seek our help for mediation. Rest assured; we shall ensure you’re compensated for justly.

6. No rude language, bullying, or sexual harassment will be tolerated under any circumstances. If found guilty, your account will be revoked, and you shall not be part of our community anymore.

PRICING POLICY

The renting price charged MUST be up to 40-50% of the retail buying price. Otherwise your product shall not be listed on our website. When an order is placed, a small flat rate of Tk.X dry-cleaning fees & Tk.Y delivery charge, and our service charge of 5% shall be charged on the transaction.

About CIRCLE

Circle is the first circular fashion brand in the country operating a peer-to-peer rental platform for clothing and accessories. Our aim is to bring a shift in the fashion industry that is not only sustainable, but also provides a way for you to both earn and save money. At the same time, we aim to give you the power of choosing your style at reasonable prices from which you get maximum value.
