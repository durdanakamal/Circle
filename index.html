HOW WE WORK

SIGN UP FOR FREE

Please note that you MUST provide identification proof: NID/ Passport/ Student ID/ Driving License.

BROWSE AND RENT

Choose the things for your favourite look and rent.

DRY CLEANING PARTNERSHIP

Your safety is our topmost priority. So, we have partnered with X Dry Cleaning Services to ensure that your rented products go through an intense dry-cleaning cycle and then sanitized before being packaged and sanitized once again for delivery.

DELIVERY SERVICE

We have partnered with the beloved Y Delivery service for swift and smooth delivery.

RETURN YOUR RENTED PRODUCT

You must return your rented product(s) within the rental time period. The rented products go through an intensive dry-cleaning cycle and sanitizing before being returned to the product owners.

SECURITY

Your security is of our utmost concern. Please follow our rules and guidelines which will be updated as the website progresses.

About CIRCLE

Circle is the first circular fashion brand in the country operating a peer-to-peer rental platform for clothing and accessories. Our aim is to bring a shift in the fashion industry that is not only sustainable, but also provides a way for you to both earn and save money. At the same time, we aim to give you the power of choosing your style at reasonable prices from which you get maximum value.
